Comment by @epage on 2022-01-11 22:23

In clap 3, we changed Vec from multiple to multiple_occurrences. This is called out in the CHANGELOG's breaking changes since structopt. This was planned in https://github.com/clap-rs/clap/issues/1772.

For reference, we also have https://github.com/clap-rs/clap/issues/3248 for this.
