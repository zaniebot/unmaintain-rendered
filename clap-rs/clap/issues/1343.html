Comment by @whoizit on 2018-09-19 05:29

Ok, I have a answer from Russian community. Just I shouldn't use load_yaml! from

let yml = load_yaml!("17_yaml.yml");
let m = App::from_yaml(yml).get_matches();

to

let yml = "17_yaml.yml";
let m = App::from_yaml(yml).get_matches();

or something like that
