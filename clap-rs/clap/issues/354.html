Currently only owned Args are passed to App, but I don't believe this required, just the way it was done.

It would be far better, and more idiomatic if one could pass in either an owned or borrowed Arg (perhaps with ~~Cow~~ ToOwned?).

Caveat, this must be done in a backwards compat way, if it can't be done and maintain backwards compat, it'll have to wait 2.x
