Comment by @AlexWaygood on 2025-09-22 12:41

I don't think we intend to support this feature. Brian gave a nice description of how mypy is able to support this here -- but to me it doesn't sound particularly consistent, and I feel like I've seen lots of issues on the mypy bug tracker from users being confused about mypy's behaviour.

Since there's precedent for our current behaviour (pyright), I'd prefer to stick with what we currently have (with some small modifications, such as https://github.com/astral-sh/ty/issues/133)
