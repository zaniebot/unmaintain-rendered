Thank you for reporting this.

The false positive is related to the TODOs for generic type aliases here (typing x as npt.NDArray[Any] makes the error go away, but should be equivalent to npt.NDArray).

To get the correct return type, we will also have to to support generic protocols in our typevar solver, I think.
