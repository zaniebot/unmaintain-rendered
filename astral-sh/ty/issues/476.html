Comment by @carljm on 2025-05-21 19:42

Our opinion is that such a rule is out of scope for a typechecker, but is a great opt-in rule to provide in a linter. For some rules this might require a type-aware linter (which we ultimately aim to provide), but disallow-untyped-defs is a much simpler rule that doesn't even require type information to provide. In fact it's already provided by Ruff! See ANN001, ANN002, and ANN003 here: https://docs.astral.sh/ruff/rules/#flake8-annotations-ann
