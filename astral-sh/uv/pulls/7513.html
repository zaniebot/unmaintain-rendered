uv: migrate to rkyv 0.8

#7513 opened by @BurntSushi on 2024-09-18 18:34
Pull request opened by @BurntSushi on 2024-09-18 18:34

Recently, rkyv 0.8 was released. Its API is a fair bit simpler now for higher level uses (like for us in uv) and results in us being able to delete a fair bit of code. This also removes our last dependency on syn 1.0, and thus drops that dependency.

Performance (via testing on the transformers example) seems to remain about the same, which is what was expected:
