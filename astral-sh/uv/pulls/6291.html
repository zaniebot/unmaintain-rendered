Invalidate uv.lock when virtual dev-dependencies change

Summary

For non-virtual workspaces, these are covered by the members. But for virtual workspaces, they aren't captured anywhere else in the lock. So, we weren't invalidating uv.lock when the dev dependencies changed, which led to a panic.
