Summary

Before this PR (and since 651fe6f4e60c0d3a789c7593c4285817d6adf932) uv depends on a git snapshot of rust-netrc at https://github.com/gribouille/netrc/commit/544f3890b621f0dc30fcefb4f804269c160ce2e9, with fixes from gribouille/netrc#3 for astral-sh/uv#8003.

Since rust-netrc 0.1.2 was just released, and it includes those fixes – plus an additional change to support ~-expansion – uv can go back to depending on published crates from crates.io.
