Summary

uv publish --dry-run will perform the --check-url validation, and hit the /validate endpoint if the registry is known to support fast-path validation (like pyx). The /validate endpoint lets us validate an upload without uploading the file contents, which lets you skip the expensive step for common mistakes.

In the future, my hope is that the /validate step will deprecated in favor of Upload API 2.0.
