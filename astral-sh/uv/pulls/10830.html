Separate musl and libc linux builds in CI

#10830 opened by @zanieb on 2025-01-21 22:07
@zanieb

We have a lot of jobs downstream of the build-binary-linux job, but the job is significantly slower than the other binary builds because we need to configure musl. Instead, we split this into two jobs (as it was before astral-sh/uv#2309#discussion_r1520101330) to speed things up.

The libc job takes ~1m and its downstream jobs finish before the musl build does. The musl job takes ~5m.
