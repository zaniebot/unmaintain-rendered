Comment by @charliermarsh on 2025-12-23 21:36

Yeah, something like:

dependencies = [
    "demucs>=4.0.1",
    "ffmpeg-python>=0.2.0",
    "librosa>=0.11.0",
    "parakeet-mlx>=0.4.1 ; sys_platform == 'darwin'",
    "torchcodec>=0.9.1",
    # more deps
]

More on the marker grammar here.
