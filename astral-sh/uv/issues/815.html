Comment by @txomon on 2024-03-05 17:48

My two cents, I would expect applications and not libraries to make more use of pip-compile-multi, as many times, running VS testing requirements are separate in these (it's been the case in all companies I have seen pip-compile / pip-compile-multi being used).

Libraries are less likely to rely on pinned dependencies (at least in python) than applications. Having requirements pinned is usually only used for tests, because runtime dependencies can't be pinned.
