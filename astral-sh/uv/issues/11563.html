UV-pack?

#11563 opened by @dipta007 on 2025-02-16 23:28
@dipta007

Summary

Can we have something like uv-pack similar to conda-pack? It will really help in reproducibility and sharing environment in the ML community, where the libs are too fast evolving and need kernel dependency.

Example

  • To pack the locak venv - uv-pack. It will pack into venv.tar.gz
  • Copy the tar to another environment
  • uv-unpack venv.tar.gz
  • Voila!! the env is reproduced
