Dependency specifier format in docs and pyproject.toml don't match

#15623 opened by @paduszyk on 2025-09-01 19:36
Issue opened by @paduszyk on 2025-09-01 19:36

I've noticed a tiny formatting inconsistency in formatting of dependency version specifiers when added with add-bounds option.

In docs:

https://github.com/astral-sh/uv/blob/9be016f3f8fdc3ac7974ed82762aa3364f6e8f2b/docs/reference/settings.md?plain=1#L744-L745

whereas in the pyproject.toml the whitespace between lower and upper bounds is not found.

This behavior is not the case of the requires-python option. If I declare requires-python = "~=3.13.7", then in uv.lock I get requires-python = ">=3.13.7, <3.14" (so the whitespace is found).
