Comment by @charliermarsh on 2024-08-09 00:59

We don't really respect --system-site-packages. We allow writing it to the virtualenv, but we don't actually query it, it just makes them available at the runtime.

I think there's no way to accomplish what you're describing. If you were using the Conda environment and installing packages etc., we'd respect those, but we don't support reading packages from another environment.

Comment by @charliermarsh on 2024-08-09 01:00

--no-dev applies when installing, not when resolving. To sync, we have to resolve, and the resolution is failing.
