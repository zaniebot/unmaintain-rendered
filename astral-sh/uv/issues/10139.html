Comment by @charliermarsh on 2024-12-24 13:02

(As an aside, you likely do not want requires-python = "==3.9". That requests exactly 3.9.0, and no later patch versions. You're probably looking for requires-python = ">=3.9" or requires-python = "==3.9.*".)
