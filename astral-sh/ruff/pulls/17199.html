#17199 opened by @sharkdp on 2025-04-04 11:11

Summary

This PR proposes a new approach to silencing unresolved-reference diagnostics by keeping track of the reachability of each use of a symbol. The changes merged in astral-sh/ruff#17169 are still needed for the "Use of variable in nested function" test case, but that could also be solved in another way eventually (see astral-sh/ty#210). We can use the same technique to silence unresolved-import and unresolved-attribute false-positives, but I think this could be merged in isolation.
