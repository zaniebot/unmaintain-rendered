Summary

Fixes https://github.com/astral-sh/ruff/issues/12916 by respecting the configured import conventions in N817.

One could argue that this is a breaking change because ET imports flagged before will now no-longer be flagged, requiring users to adopt their configuration if they want to keep flagging ET. I think it's fine but interested in more opinions.
