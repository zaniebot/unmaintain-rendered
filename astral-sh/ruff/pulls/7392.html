Avoid flagging single-quoted docstrings with continuations for multi-line rules

#7392 opened by @charliermarsh on 2023-09-14 18:48
@charliermarsh

Rules like D209 and D205 are only intended to apply to multi-line docstrings. If a docstring is single-quoted, but extends via a continuation, it should be excluded (it'll be flagged by another rule anyway). Closes astral-sh/ruff#7058.
