Summary

We already avoid fixing B007 if the loop variable appears to be used beyond the loop body. This PR makes the logic a bit more conservative, also avoiding the fix for usages of any shadowed bindings (since we can't know for certain whether the shadowing binding actually took affect, as in the else: bar = 1 case seen in the linked issue).

Closes #4599.
