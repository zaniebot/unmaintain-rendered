[red-knot] Heterogeneous tuple types with differently ordered (but equivalent) unions at the same index should be considered equivalent

#15637 opened by @AlexWaygood on 2025-01-21 12:46
@AlexWaygood
Summary

Fixes astral-sh/ruff#15636.

We considered int | str equivalent to str | int, but we failed to understand that tuple[int | str] was equivalent to tuple[str | int]. The property tests caught this, because we did understand that tuple[int | str] was gradually equivalent to tuple[str | int], and the property tests realised that there was an internal inconsistency here since tuple[int | str] and tuple[str | int] are both fully static types.
