[ty] Stabilize rename

#21940 opened by @MichaReiser on 2025-12-12 11:02
@MichaReiser
Summary

Stabilizes the rename functionality and removes the experimental.rename option.

I decided to keep the Experimental options section around for now because I forsee us adding new experimental features in the future and having to redo all this boilerplate seems annoying.

This PR also fixes a bug where rename didn't work in notebooks because we didn't consider virtual files (files that are just open but have no path) to be part of the project.

Closes astral-sh/ty#1784
