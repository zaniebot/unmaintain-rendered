[numpy] numpy-legacy-random

#2960 opened by @sbrugman on 2023-02-16 17:06
@sbrugman

The new Generator in NumPy uses bits provided by PCG64 which has better statistical properties than the legacy MT19937 used in RandomState. Global random functions can also be problematic with parallel processing.

This rule is probably quite useful for data scientists (perhaps in combination with nbqa)
