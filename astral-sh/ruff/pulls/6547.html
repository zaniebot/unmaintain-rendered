Remove lex and parsing from formatter benchmark

#6547 opened by @MichaReiser on 2023-08-14 06:22
@MichaReiser

Summary

This PR changes the formatter benchmark to only benchmark the formatting time by excluding the lexing and parsing time.

Excluding the lexing and parsing time (which make up more than 50% of the whole formatting time) allows us to more cleary identify performance changes in the formatter itself. It further helps to gather profiles that don't include the whole parsing noise.
