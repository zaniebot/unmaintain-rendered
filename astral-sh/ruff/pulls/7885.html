Allow bindings to be created and referenced within annotations

#7885 opened by @charliermarsh on 2023-10-10 03:42
Pull request opened by @charliermarsh on 2023-10-10 03:42

Summary

Given:

baz: Annotated[
    str,
    [qux for qux in foo],
]

We treat baz as BindingKind::Annotation, to ensure that references to baz are marked as unbound. However, we were also treating qux as BindingKind::Annotation, which meant that the load in the comprehension also errored.

Closes https://github.com/astral-sh/ruff/issues/7879.
