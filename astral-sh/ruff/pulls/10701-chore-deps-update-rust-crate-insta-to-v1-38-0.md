### [`v1.38.0`](https://togithub.com/mitsuhiko/insta/blob/HEAD/CHANGELOG.md#1380)

-   Vendored old `yaml-rust` dependency to avoid rustsec warnings.  [#&#8203;465](https://togithub.com/mitsuhiko/insta/issues/465)

### [`v1.36.1`](https://togithub.com/mitsuhiko/insta/blob/HEAD/CHANGELOG.md#1361)

[Compare Source](https://togithub.com/mitsuhiko/insta/compare/1.36.0...1.36.1)

-   Fix an ownership issue introduced in 1.36 with snapshot assertions.  [#&#8203;453](https://togithub.com/mitsuhiko/insta/issues/453)

### [`v1.36.0`](https://togithub.com/mitsuhiko/insta/blob/HEAD/CHANGELOG.md#1360)

[Compare Source](https://togithub.com/mitsuhiko/insta/compare/1.35.1...1.36.0)

-   Deprecate `INSTA_FORCE_UPDATE_SNAPSHOTS` env-var for `INSTA_FORCE_UPDATE`.
    The latter was documented, the former was implemented.  [#&#8203;449](https://togithub.com/mitsuhiko/insta/issues/449)

-   Add `require_full_match` option.  [#&#8203;448](https://togithub.com/mitsuhiko/insta/issues/448)

-   Deprecate `assert_display_snapshot!`.  [#&#8203;385](https://togithub.com/mitsuhiko/insta/issues/385)
