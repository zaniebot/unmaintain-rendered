Hmm, that breaks things, and I think I know why: an empty export list (__all__ = []) is semantically different to a module not defining __all__ at all. __all__ = [] means there are no public re-exports from the module; not defining __all__ at all means everything is publicly re-exported from the module (unless it has a name starting with a single underscore).
