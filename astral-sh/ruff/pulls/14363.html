Remove the optional salsa dependency from the AST crate

#14363 opened by @MichaReiser on 2024-11-15 16:40

Summary

I added the Lookup implementation to Name to allow Class and FunctionType lookups with a &str. What I didn't realize is that all lookups have a &Name reference. We never have a case where we just have a &str.

That's why I'm removing the Lookup implementation because I prefer if ruff-python-ast doesn't depend on salsa, even if optionally.
