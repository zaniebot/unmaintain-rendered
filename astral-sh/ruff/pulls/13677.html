Fixes https://github.com/astral-sh/ruff/issues/13668

Luckily, the lint.allow_unused_imports setting isn't read anywhere. However, this could still be considered a breaking change because Ruff might fail to read a previously valid configuration. I do think that it should be very unlikely, considering that the configuration was "useless" before.
