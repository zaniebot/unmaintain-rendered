Summary

This PR adds a new AnyFunctionDefinition enum that abstracts over StmtFunctionDef and StmtAsyncFunctionDef.

Having a union that abstracts over both variants is useful in cases where the linter's or formatters behavior doesn't rely on the fact whether it is an async function or not.
