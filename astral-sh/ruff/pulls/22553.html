So this is basically a textbook case of https://github.com/astral-sh/ty/issues/1578. This PR makes our if type(x) is y narrowing more consistent with our narrowing elsewhere, and gets rid of undesirable Unknowns and strange types like list[Unknown] & list[str] like we have on main.
