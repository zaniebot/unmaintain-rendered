Remove pyproject.toml from fixtures directory

#8726 opened by @charliermarsh on 2023-11-16 17:32
Pull request opened by @charliermarsh on 2023-11-16 17:32

Summary

This exists to power a test, but it ends up affecting the behavior of all files in the directory. Namely, it means that these files aren't excluded when you format or lint them directly, since in that case, Ruff will fall back to looking at the pyproject.toml in crates/ruff_linter/resources/test/fixtures, which doesn't exclude these files, unlike our top-level pyproject.toml.
