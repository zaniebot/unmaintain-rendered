[ty] Don't show hover for expressions with no inferred type

#21924 opened by @MichaReiser on 2025-12-11 17:18
@MichaReiser
Summary

ty doesn't infer types for every expression. This PR ensures we don't show Unknown when hovering such an expression but, instead, don't show a hover at all.

Whether we want to show a different hover in this case is something I'll suggest leaving for another PR.
