Summary

This PR stabilizes the preview behavior for invalid-argument-name (N803) to ignore argument names of functions decorated with typing.override because these methods are out of the authors control.

This behavior was introduced in https://github.com/astral-sh/ruff/pull/15954 and released as part of Ruff 0.9.5 (6th of February).

There have been no new issues or PRs since this behavior change (preview) was introduced.
