dependencies: switch from chrono to jiff

#17376 opened by @BurntSushi on 2025-04-13 22:24
@BurntSushi

We weren't really using chrono for anything other than getting the current time and formatting it for logs. So the switch to Jiff here is pretty uninteresting.

Unfortunately, this doesn't quite get us to a point where chrono can be removed. From what I can tell, we're still bringing it via tracing-subscriber and quick-junit. tracing-subscriber does have an issue open about Jiff, but there's no movement on it.

Normally I'd suggest holding off on this since it doesn't get us all of the way there and it would be better to avoid bringing in two datetime libraries, but we are, it appears, already there. In particular, env_logger brings in Jiff. So this PR doesn't really make anything worse, but it does bring us closer to an all-Jiff world.
