Update ERA100 to apply to commented dictionary items with trailing comments

#6822 opened by @zanieb on 2023-08-23 17:30
Pull request opened by @zanieb on 2023-08-23 17:30

Closes https://github.com/astral-sh/ruff/issues/6821

ERA100 was not raising on commented parts of dictionaries if it included another comment (such as a noqa clause). In cases where this comment was a noqa clause, RUF100 to be emitted since the noqa would have no effect. Here, we update ERA100 to raise even when there are trailing comments. This resolves the linked issue and increases the scope of ERA100. We could narrow the regular expression to only apply to noqa comments if we do not want to expand ERA100 however I think this change is within the spirit of the rule.
