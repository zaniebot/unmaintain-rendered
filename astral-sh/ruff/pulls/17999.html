Skip S608 for expressionless f-strings

#17999 opened by @maxmynter on 2025-05-10 04:02
@maxmynter

Closes: #17967

Summary

Skip S608 for expressionless f-strings. Expressionless f-strings are not vulnerable to SQL injections and F541 catches expressionless f-strings with an autofix.

This is achieved with guarding on the S608 f-string match with an occurrence check for expressions within it; analogous to the implementation of F451.
