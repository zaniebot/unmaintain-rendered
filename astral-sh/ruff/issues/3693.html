Comment by @akx on 2023-03-28 12:25

not sure if other cultures often use commas in large numbers other than ever three digits?

At least the Indian numbering system uses a "[2,...]2,2,3" grouping; quoting the examples from the linked Wikipedia page:

5,00,000 <=> 500,000 12,34,56,789 <=> 123,456,789 17,00,00,00,000 <=> 17,000,000,000 6,78,90,00,00,00,00,000 <=> 6,789,000,000,000,000
