Comment by @charliermarsh on 2023-10-31 21:21

To ignore a rule across a whole file, try adding # ruff: noqa: F401 on its own line, e.g., at the top of the file.

The # noqa: F401 syntax (without the # ruff prefix) is used to suppress errors inline, so you'd add that at the end of a specific line that contains the F401 error to suppress it.
