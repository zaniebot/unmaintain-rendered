I don't think it makes sense, because there's - [banned-import-from (ICN003)](https://docs.astral.sh/ruff/rules/banned-import-from/#banned-import-from-icn003) and [unconventional-import-alias (ICN001)](https://docs.astral.sh/ruff/rules/unconventional-import-alias/#unconventional-import-alias-icn001)

```toml
[tool.ruff.lint.flake8-import-conventions.extend-aliases]
"datetime" = "dt"
"time" = "tm"

[tool.ruff.lint.flake8-import-conventions]
banned-from = ["datetime", "time"]
```
