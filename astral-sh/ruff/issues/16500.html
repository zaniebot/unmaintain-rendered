Summary

Ruff triggers bad preview behavior of C409. This behavior we chose not to enable because of issue: astral-sh/ruff#12912 . This is definitely a false positive in this case and should not be reported here as it does lead to a significant performance regression. See this issue for discussion: pytorch/pytorch#148412
