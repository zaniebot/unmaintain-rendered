YTT201 doesn’t check sys.version_info.major

#18958 opened by @dscorbett on 2025-06-26 13:01
@dscorbett
Summary

sys-version-info0-eq3 (YTT201) checks sys.version_info[0] but not the synonymous sys.version_info.major. There could either be a new YTT rule (by analogy with YTT203 and YTT204, two rules to check the synonymous sys.version_info[1] and sys.version_info.minor) or an expansion of YTT201 (as in #18633, which expanded the scope of outdated-version-block (UP036)).
