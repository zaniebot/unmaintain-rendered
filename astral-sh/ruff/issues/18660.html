PLW0128 skips assignment targets in square brackets and after asterisks

Closed #18660 opened by @dscorbett on 2025-06-13 12:32
Issue opened by @dscorbett on 2025-06-13 12:32

Summary

redeclared-assigned-name (PLW0128) has false negatives for assignment targets in square brackets or after asterisks. It already correctly checks within parentheses.

$ cat >plw0128.py <<'# EOF'
x, *x = 1, 2
[x, x] = 1, 2
# EOF

$ ruff --isolated check plw0128.py --select PLW0128
All checks passed!
