Would love to see support for the flake8-use-pathlib plugin for enforcing use of pathlib over os.

https://gitlab.com/RoPP/flake8-use-pathlib https://pypi.org/project/flake8-use-pathlib/

For justification on why using pathlib is "better" than os.

https://realpython.com/python-pathlib/ https://treyhunner.com/2018/12/why-you-should-be-using-pathlib/ https://treyhunner.com/2019/01/no-really-pathlib-is-great/

os.path operations and their pathlib equivalents

https://docs.python.org/3/library/pathlib.html#correspondence-to-tools-in-the-os-module
