add-trailing-comma also has unhug-trailing-paren, unhug-leading-paren and avoids arbitrary indentation. Which is not enforced by Ruff or Autopep8. Even Black only enforces it when magic commas are used:

print(1,
      2,
      3,
      4,
      5)

print(
    1,
    2,
    3,
    4,
    5,
