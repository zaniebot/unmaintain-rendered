[rule proposal] avoid creating temporary lists

Open #6270 opened by @dorschw on 2023-08-02 11:22
Issue opened by @dorschw on 2023-08-02 11:22
- any([foo,bar])
+ any((foo,bar))

- all([foo,bar])
+ all((foo,bar))

- ",".join([foo,bar])
+ ",".join((foo,bar))

No reason to create a mutable list temporarily, where a tuple can be used. Not sure whether it should change temporary sets to tuples in the any/all cases. (definitely not in the join case)

Somewhat related: https://github.com/astral-sh/ruff/issues/6176
