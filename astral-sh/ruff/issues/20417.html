Comment by @dylwil3 on 2025-09-15 14:06

One reason would be to ensure that you and your collaborators are using the same version of Ruff. Also note that if you manage your monorepo as a uv workspace then you could set the dependency for all of your packages at once, uniformly.

Comment by @dylwil3 on 2025-09-15 14:19

Not at this time, no - Ruff performs (with the exception of some rules that look at your directory structure) single-file analysis (as opposed to type-checkers like mypy or ty)
