These two rules do the exact same thing but, have slightly different logic. They should be an alias of one another but appear to each be there own rule: https://docs.astral.sh/ruff/rules/repeated-isinstance-calls/ and https://docs.astral.sh/ruff/rules/duplicate-isinstance-call/ do the same thing, and have the same autofix implemented. Seems like this wasn't notice when the PR was merged astral-sh/ruff#4792
