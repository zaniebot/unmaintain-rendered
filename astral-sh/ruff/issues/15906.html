Description

Title intentionally ridiculous ... The options are getting pretty convoluted.

With ruff format you can (1) request auto-fix (default) or (2) check that said auto-fixes were applied (--check). WIth ruff check you can (1) request auto-fix (--fix-only) but NOT (2) check that said auto-fixes were applied.

Rationale: run (1) in pre-commit and (2) in CI.
