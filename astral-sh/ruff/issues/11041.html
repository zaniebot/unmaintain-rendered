Comment by @MichaReiser on 2024-04-19 13:57

Ruff only supports fmt: skip at the statement level

[
    ...
    TextItem(
        "<speak>    Привет    как дела   ? </speak>",
        ParsedItem(
            ["п", "р'", "и", "в'", "е+", "т", "\t", "к", "а+", "к", "\t", "д'", "и", "л", "а+", "\t", "?"],
        ),
    ),
    ...
]  # fmt: skip

You can enable RUF028 to get warned about incompatible formatter suppression comments.
