walk: treat symbolic links to directories as directories

#1397 opened by @nikofil on 2019-10-02 09:17
@nikofil

Due to how walkdir works if symlinks are not followed, symlinks to directories are seen as simple files by ripgrep. This caused a panic in some cases due to receiving a WalkEvent::Exit event without a corresponding WalkEvent::Dir event.

This is fixed by looking at the metadata of the file in the case of a symlink to determine if it's a directory.

Fixes #1389
