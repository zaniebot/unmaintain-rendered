I hope this is the idiomatic way to do this. Seems to work at least:

% ls
abc  bbc  cbc  dbc

# Old behaviour
% \rg -u --files -g '[!ab]bc'
cbc
dbc
% \rg -u --files -g '[^ab]bc'
abc
bbc

# New behaviour
% rg-dev -u --files -g '[!ab]bc'
cbc
dbc
% rg-dev -u --files -g '[^ab]bc'
cbc
dbc
