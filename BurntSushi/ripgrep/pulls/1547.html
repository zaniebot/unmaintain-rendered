grep-cli: Support files compressed by compress(1)

#1547 opened by @mineo on 2020-04-10 09:27
@mineo

While Linux distributions (at least Arch Linux, RHEL, Debian) do not support compressing files with compress(1), macOS & AIX do (the utility is part of POSIX). Additionally, gzip is able to uncompress such compressed files and provides an uncompress binary.
