They actually provided the --debug output in the gist, and it says:

glob converted to regex: Glob { glob: "**/'*'", re: "(?-u)^(?:/?|.*/)'[^/]*'$" [...]

So yeah, the single quotes are interpreted literally and they end up in the glob pattern.
