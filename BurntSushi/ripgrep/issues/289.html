Comment by @BurntSushi on 2016-12-23 20:16

I was on mobile before. Here's a more useful workaround:

$ cat $HOME/bin/rgp
#!/bin/sh

exec rg -p "$@" | less -RFX

Then you can just use rgp whenever you want to have a pager pause results for you.
