Comment by @BurntSushi on 2023-02-16 16:05

As explained in the man page and the GUIDE, every single line in a config file is its own argument. So when you do --ignore-file foo, ripgrep sees that as one single argument. You can fix it by either joining them with a =, i.e., --ignore-file=foo, or by putting each on their own line:

--ignore-file
foo
