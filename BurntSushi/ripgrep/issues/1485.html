What version of ripgrep are you using?

Official amd64 .deb package.

Ubuntu 19.10 x64

Describe your question, feature request, or bug.

The fish completions conflict with the completions provided by fish's official Ubuntu (.deb) package, https://launchpad.net/~fish-shell/+archive/ubuntu/release-3 , causing installation to fail.

Fish devs suggest at fish-shell/fish-shell#5822#issuecomment-585908454 that 3rd-party completions should be packaged to /usr/share/fish/vendor_completions.d instead of /usr/share/fish/completions, which is fish's sole kingdom.
