# YTT201 doesn’t check `sys.version_info.major`

---

_Issue opened by @dscorbett on 2025-06-26 13:01_

### Summary

[`sys-version-info0-eq3` (YTT201)](https://docs.astral.sh/ruff/rules/sys-version-info0-eq3/) checks `sys.version_info[0]` but not the synonymous `sys.version_info.major`. There could either be a new YTT rule (by analogy with [YTT203](https://docs.astral.sh/ruff/rules/sys-version-info1-cmp-int/) and [YTT204](https://docs.astral.sh/ruff/rules/sys-version-info-minor-cmp-int/), two rules to check the synonymous `sys.version_info[1]` and `sys.version_info.minor`) or an expansion of YTT201 (as in #18633, which expanded the scope of [`outdated-version-block` (UP036)](https://docs.astral.sh/ruff/rules/outdated-version-block/)).
